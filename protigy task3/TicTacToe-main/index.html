Tic Tac Toe

Rules of the game

  • Grid: The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
  • Marks: Two players take turns marking spaces in the grid with their respective symbols, usually "X" and "O"
  • Winning Condition: A player wins the game if they successfully place three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row.
  • Draw Condition: If all spaces on the grid are filled and no player has achieved a winning condition, the game ends in a draw.
